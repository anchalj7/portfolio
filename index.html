About Me

Web Developer

Anchal Jaiswal is an enthusiastic and driven diploma student in Computer Science Engineering at Lovely Professional University, currently maintaining an impressive CGPA of 8.74. With a strong foundation in programming languages like Java, JavaScript, and C, she also excels in modern web technologies such as React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, HTML, CSS, and MySQL. Her technical skills are supported by hands-on experience in full-stack development, gained through her internship at O7 Services, where she developed dynamic websites using PHP, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. Anchal has worked on impactful projects, including **CharityLoom**, a web platform designed to facilitate the donation of food, clothes, and services. She also developed a **Healthcare App** using Java and Android Studio, focused on health tracking and patient care with a user-friendly UI/UX. These projects reflect her ability to combine technical skills with social impact. Certified in web development and Android app development, she demonstrates a keen interest in continuous learning and innovation. Recognized on the University Honor Roll for academic excellence, Anchal is also active in extracurricular activities and cultural events. With strong problem-solving abilities, a creative mindset, and a collaborative spirit, she is eager to contribute to dynamic teams and meaningful tech-driven solutions.
